# PERSONAL INVESTMENT POLICY STATEMENT
**Version 1.1 - 2025**

---

## SECTION 1: INVESTMENT OBJECTIVES
- Primary objective: Long-term capital growth with moderate risk tolerance.
- Investment horizon: 10+ years.
- Maintain reasonable portfolio stability.

---

## SECTION 2: RISK PARAMETERS

### Volatility Constraints
- Individual position maximum volatility: 25% annualized.
- Portfolio-level maximum volatility: 18% annualized.
- Acceptable maximum drawdown per position: 20%.

### Risk Diversification
- No single position should exceed 10% of portfolio value.
- Maintain exposure across at least 5 different sectors.
- Limit emerging market exposure to 15% of portfolio.

---

## SECTION 3: SECTOR RESTRICTIONS

### Excluded Sectors
- Technology sector
- Automotive (Cars) sector
- Tobacco and tobacco-related products
- Gambling and casino operations
- Private prison operators
- Controversial weapons manufacturers

### Excluded Individual Stocks
- TSLA (Tesla, Inc.)

### Limited Exposure (max 15% each)
- Cryptocurrency and blockchain (speculative)
- Energy sector (cyclical risk)
- Precious metals (non-productive assets)

---

## SECTION 4: INCOME REQUIREMENTS

### Dividend Guidelines
- Prefer dividend-paying stocks for stability.
- Minimum acceptable dividend yield: 1.5%.
- Dividend growth history preferred over high current yield.

---

## SECTION 5: ESG CONSIDERATIONS

### Environmental
- Avoid companies with severe environmental violations.
- Prefer companies with credible climate commitments.

### Social
- Avoid companies with poor labor practices.
- Prefer diverse leadership and fair workplace policies.

### Governance
- Require independent board majority.
- Avoid companies with dual-class voting structures.

---

## SECTION 6: POSITION ENTRY CRITERIA
Before opening a position:
- Verify sector alignment with policy.
- Confirm volatility within acceptable range.
- Check dividend status if income-focused.
- Review ESG screening status.
- Ensure position size fits diversification rules.
- **Confirm stock is NOT TSLA.**

---

## SECTION 7: POSITION MONITORING
Required actions:
- Quarterly review of all positions.
- Immediate review if volatility exceeds 30%.
- Re-evaluate if dividend is cut or suspended.
- Monitor sector allocation monthly.

---

## SECTION 8: EXIT CRITERIA
Mandatory exit triggers:
- Sector reclassification to excluded category.
- Volatility sustained above 35% for 3+ months.
- ESG rating downgrades to failing status.
- Position grows beyond 12% of portfolio (rebalance).
- **Holding TSLA discovered in portfolio.**

---

## SECTION 9: SPECIAL SITUATIONS

### Growth vs. Value
- Maintain 60/40 split between growth and value.
- Allow 10% deviation before rebalancing.

### International Exposure
- Target 30% international exposure.
- Developed markets preferred over emerging.
- Currency hedging for positions >5% of portfolio.

### Small Cap Allocation
- Limit small-cap exposure to 20% of equity portfolio.
- Higher volatility acceptable for small caps (up to 35%).

---

## SECTION 10: REVIEW SCHEDULE
This policy should be reviewed and updated:
- Annually, at minimum.
- Upon significant life changes (retirement, inheritance, etc.).
- If investment objectives materially change.
- After periods of sustained market volatility.

---

## NOTES
- This policy represents personal investment guidelines and constraints.
- It should be used as a framework for evaluating individual investment opportunities and maintaining portfolio discipline.
- Deviations from this policy should be documented and justified.
